Viash-Hub (in development - 2024)

Unlock The Power of Knowledge with Viash-hub

Effective knowledge management is crucial for organizations to maintain a competitive edge and drive innovation.

At Data Intuitive, we understand the value of leveraging the knowledge and expertise of your R&D team and are developing Viash-hub as a tool to capture this knowledge and facilitate sharing and dissemination.

Viash-hub will be a collaboration platform for data workflows in biotech R&D, designed to centralize modules and workflows and facilitate collaboration across departments and organizations. With Viash-hub, our clients will easily access and utilize the knowledge and expertise of their team and benefit from the efficiencies and insights gained through the sharing of best practices and proven solutions.

By fostering a culture of knowledge sharing and continuous learning, we believe that Viash-hub can help our clients stay at the forefront of their field and drive progress through data-driven decision making.

Public Viash-hub (2024)

Viash-hub public will allow academics, pharmaceutical R&D and research centers to share their knowledge and collaborate on public data workflows via a publicly available instance of the platform, which can help facilitate the sharing and dissemination of research findings and encourage collaboration. It will be available free of charge on the internet, making it more accessible to researchers and organizations.
